Frances Ink designs and builds websites.

Frances Ink has been building sites for small businesses and nonprofits since 2010. We work on small one-time projects and maintain sites as part of a team.

Technologies and platforms are continually changing. We work with clients to find the best technical and design fit, from boutique shops serving local artisan communities to national activist organizations coordinating chapters across the country.

Based in Alexandria, Virginia, Frances Ink works with clients across the US. Give Jenine Drew a call to discuss your needs!!

Jenine Drew leads Frances Ink. After building the website for her family’s land development company, Jenine decided she loved coding and made the leap, starting Frances Ink as a side project while continuing to operate heavy equipment during the day.

All these years later, Frances Ink is a going concern and Jenine wears sweat pants instead of Carhartts (she works from home).

Experience working in a small business shapes my approach to every project. Before making any technical recommendations, it’s important to understand the organization’s overall goals and resources.

When not staring at screens, Jenine enjoys going outside with her two hound dogs, Ernesto and Ladybird.
